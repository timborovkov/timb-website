## LinkedIn — About

~2,530 chars (LinkedIn limit: 2,600)

```
Software engineer by craft, classical liberal and European federalist 🇪🇺 by conviction. Shipping code since I was 12, building products and companies ever since.

I co-run Tecci, a software consultancy I've been running with my partners since 2019. We build enterprise web apps, commerce platforms, and integrations for Nordic companies like Puumerkki, Faba, and Cushman & Wakefield. I lead engineering there, drive product development, and run projects from scope to ship.

I also co-founded UpCharge, Finland's leading power bank sharing network, and served as its founding CEO — running operations, growth, investor relations, and product, while leading development of the mobile app, PWA, backend, and admin tools that power thousands of users across hundreds of venues. In early 2025 I stepped back from the active business role to go deeper on the AI wave: focusing on building new products and technologies like IRMIN, and spending a couple of months in San Francisco to see firsthand what the industry's leaders are working on and the problems they're solving. I came back more inspired and determined to build than ever.

How I work: end-to-end. Architecture, DevOps, data pipelines, backend, frontend, and the LLM and agent layer reshaping all of it. Self-taught and full-stack in the broadest sense — comfortable from infra to UI to team lead. Main stack today is TypeScript and Go; I started in Java and passed through Swift on the way.

The past couple of years have been defined by deep learning and growth: AI, LLMs, data, and leveling up as an engineer, architect, and founder. I work seven days a week, 80+ hours, and try never to end a day without shipping something.

About nine months a year I work remotely while traveling — coding from cafés, beaches, and anywhere with decent wifi. When I'm not on the road, I split time between Helsinki and Tallinn.

Outside of work: karaoke, ebike rides, craft beer, techno, and the occasional weekend lost in grand strategy games. I also geek out on parliamentary procedure and political debates — as teens with friends we used to simulate parliaments in spreadsheets all night for fun.

On the political side: I'm a classical liberal and European federalist. I advocate for free-market liberalism, open societies, and a more united, sovereign Europe — and I believe tech is one of our most underused levers.

Always happy to hear from founders, builders, developers, engineers, collaborators, and anyone curious about tech, startups, or European politics.
```
